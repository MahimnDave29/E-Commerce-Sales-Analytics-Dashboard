# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive Excel dashboard for analyzing e-commerce sales performance, providing insights into sales trends, profitability, geographic distribution, and product category performance.

## Dashboard Overview

This interactive dashboard provides real-time insights into key e-commerce metrics and business performance indicators across multiple dimensions including time, geography, product categories and segments.

## Key Metrics

The dashboard tracks the following primary KPIs:

- **Total Sales**: $22.97M
- **Total Profit**: $2.86M
- **Quantity Sold**: 37,873 units
- **Number of Orders**: 9,994
- **Profit Margin**: 12.47%

### Year-over-Year Growth
- Sales: + 20.62%
- Profit: + 14.41%
- Quantity Sold: + 27.42%
- Orders: + 28.64%
- Profit Margin: - 5.15%

## Features

### 1. Sales and Profit Analysis
- Monthly trend analysis with dual-axis visualization
- Comparative view of sales volume vs. profitability
- Identifies seasonal patterns and peak performance periods

### 2. Category Performance
- **Category-wise Profit**: Breakdown across Technology ($122.49K), Office Supplies ($18.45K), and Furniture ($145.45K)
- **Sales Share Distribution**: 
  - Furniture: 36.40%
  - Technology: 32.30%
  - Office Supplies: 31.30%

### 3. Geographic Analysis
- Interactive US state map visualization
- Sales distribution ranging from $0.92K to $457.69K by state
- Enables regional performance comparison and territory optimization

### 4. Subcategory Insights
Top 5 performing subcategories by sales:
1. Phones: $330.01K
2. Chairs: $328.45K
3. Storage: $223.84K
4. Tables: $206.97K
5. Binders: $203.41K

### 5. Dynamic Filtering
Multi-dimensional filtering capabilities:
- **Segment**: Consumer, Corporate, Home Office
- **Region**: Central, East, South, West
- **Year**: 2011-2014

## Use Cases

1. **Executive Reporting**: Quick overview of business health and growth trends
2. **Sales Performance Tracking**: Monitor sales targets and identify growth opportunities
3. **Regional Strategy**: Identify high-performing regions and growth markets
4. **Profitability Analysis**: Track profit margins and identify improvement areas

## Key Insights

- Strong YoY growth across most metrics (20%+ in sales and orders)
- Technology and Furniture categories drive the majority of revenue
- Phones and Chairs are top-performing subcategories
- Geographic sales distribution shows significant regional variations
- Consistent profitability maintained at ~12.5% margin however there has been decrease of 5.15% in profit margins YoY.
